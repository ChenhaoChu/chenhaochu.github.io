Bio

I am currently currently a postdoctoral research fellow, supervised by Prof. Dr. Hua Wang, at Department of Information Technology and Electrical Engineering (D-ITET) of Swiss Federal Institute of Technology Zürich (ETH Zürich), Zurich, Switzerland. From October 2017 to September 2018, he was a Research Assistant with the State Key Laboratory of Millimeter Waves, Department of Electronic Engineering, CityU. I am a member of Social Media Subcommittee in Electronic Information Committee of IEEE Microwave Theory and Technology Society (MTT-S).

My research interests lie at the intersection broadband high-efficiency power amplifiers/transmitters at RF/mm-Wave, MMIC power amplifier design for RF/mm-wave applications, and antenna-in-package (AiP) for phased arrays. I have a great passion on new things and ideas, my goal is to create magic and happiness. Outside my research, I love photography, badminton, and movie appreciation.
